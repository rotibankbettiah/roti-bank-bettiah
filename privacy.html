Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 18, 2026

Roti Bank Bettiah Trust ("we", "us", or "our") is a registered charitable food NGO and trust (Reg No. 5071/2023) based in Bettiah, West Champaran, Bihar, India. We are dedicated to providing free daily meals to the underprivileged, homeless, and needy.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, process, and protect your personal information when you visit our website (rotibankbettiah.org), donate to our cause, subscribe to our newsletter, apply for internships, or volunteer with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information directly from you when you interact with our website, including:

  • Donor Details: Name, email address, phone number, and PAN card number (required for issuing 80G tax-exemption receipts under Indian Income Tax law).
  • Payment Information: All online transactions are processed securely through our verified payment gateway partner, Razorpay. We do not store or process your credit/debit card details, UPI IDs, or net banking credentials on our servers.
  • Volunteer & Internship Applications: Contact details, educational background, address, and any information submitted through our application forms.
  • Newsletter Subscription: Your email address when you voluntarily sign up for our newsletters or updates.
  • Usage Data: Non-personally identifiable technical information such as IP address, browser type, device details, and site usage statistics collected through analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data for legitimate purposes, including:

  • Processing your financial contributions and sending donation receipts.
  • Filing tax-exemption returns (80G) with the Income Tax Department of India for eligible donations.
  • Communicating news, operational updates, and details about how your donations are utilized (with an option to opt-out at any time).
  • Reviewing and processing volunteer or internship applications.
  • Improving our website performance, layout, and user experience.
  • Preventing fraudulent transactions and maintaining the security of our platform.

3. Data Security & Storage

We prioritize the safety of your personal information. We utilize Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) encryption for transmitting data on our website.

Your personal information is stored securely in our hosting databases (Supabase / Cloudflare). Access to this data is restricted to authorized personnel who need the information to perform specific administrative tasks (such as preparing accounts or generating certificates).

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We only share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our NGO, including:

  • Razorpay Software Private Limited: To securely capture, authorize, and verify online payment transactions.
  • Supabase, Inc. & Cloudflare, Inc.: For hosting, cloud database storage, and securing web endpoints.
  • Resend: To safely transmit newsletter confirmation emails and automated receipts.

All third-party service providers are contractually obligated to handle your information securely and strictly in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

5. Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information if required to do so by Indian law, a court order, or a government authority, specifically to comply with the regulations of the **Income Tax Department** or under the **Information Technology Act, 2000** (and its subsequent amendments).

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • Access & Correction: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you or request updates to incorrect details.
  • Erasure: You can request that we delete your personal information from our active databases, subject to any statutory record-keeping regulations (e.g., audit requirements for financial transactions under Indian tax laws).
  • Opt-Out: You can unsubscribe from our newsletters or update lists by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email or by contacting us directly.
